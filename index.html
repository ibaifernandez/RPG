Physical Attributes

Here, you get to choose your physical attributes. A zero-level attribute can be achieved thru illness, magic, potions, accidents, but not in its creation. It's up to the player to keep playing the game with a zero-level attribute character.

  • Your strength determines your brute force — the one you use to throw something as far as possible or to tighten a knot.
  • Your dexterity determines your capability to handle things gracefully — like using weapons or climbing a tree.
  • Your stamina determines how much physical effort and damage you're able to resist.

Important: You're up to distribute 6 points in total.

Value legend
  1. Under average - You are not as good or capable as the rest of living (or undead) beings who surround you.
  2. Average - You are just as good or capable as most of living beings (or undead) around you.
  3. Above average - You are slightly better or more capable than the the rest of living beings (or undead) who surround you.
  4. Excellent - You are much better or much more capable than many of the living (or undead) beings around you.
  5. Master - You are so incredibly better or more capable than the rest of living (or undead) beings who you coexist with.

Social Attributes

Here, you get to choose your social attributes:

  • Your charisma determines how likeable you are once you start interacting with living (or undead) beings — in a conversation or a public speech.
  • Your looks determines how much you match the beauty standards of your kingdom (as well as of others).
  • Your vibe determines how you are regarded without having to engage in any sort of interaction with your peers.

Mental Attributes

Here, you get to choose your mental attributes:

  • Your intelligence determines how well your brain works in matters of knowledge acquisition, knowledge processing, memory...
  • Your wits determines how clever you are in terms unrelated to knowledge and/or memory.
  • Your perception determines how sensitive you're regarding your five senses.
